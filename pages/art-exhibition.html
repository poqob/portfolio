Art Exhibition Application

An art and art literature application

About the Project

Art Exhibition is a comprehensive mobile application focused on art and art literature. Users can explore museums, artworks, and artists, save content, and customize application settings.

Developed with Flutter, this application offers a modern and user-friendly interface for art enthusiasts.

Features

  • Museum catalogs (grid and page views)
  • Artwork collections
  • Artist profiles and information
  • Content saving and favorites
  • Account and application settings
  • User-friendly login and registration system

Technologies Used

Dart Flutter C++ CMake PostgreSQL
